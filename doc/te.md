# Stakeholder: Test Engineering

## Definition
Represents the responsibility perspective for product and system correctness before release.

## Core Responsibility
Ensuring defects and regressions are detected as close to their origin as possible.

## Owned Risks
- Late defect discovery
- False confidence through insufficient testing
- Gaps between specification and behavior

## Desired Outcomes
- Failures surface early and locally
- Test results are trustworthy signals
- Quality feedback is actionable

## Trade-off Authority
- Test depth vs. execution speed
- Automation vs. manual validation

## Non-Goals
- Product direction
- Architectural ownership
- Release scheduling

## Relationship to Roles
- Test engineer role
- Test design role

## Notes on Conflict
Often constrained by upstream decisions outside its control.
